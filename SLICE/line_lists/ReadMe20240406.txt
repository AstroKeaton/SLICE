Byte-by-byte Description of file
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Bytes Format  Units   Explanations
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   1-  6  A6     ---     Model name
   8- 24  F17.14 micron  Wavelength in vacuum
  26- 33  A8     ---     Species
  35- 74  A40    ---     Transition information
  76- 84  E9.3   s-1     Einstein A-coefficient
  86- 96  F11.1  K       Upper level term energy
  98-147  A50    ---     Comments, including the information on the dust feature
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

References
For the atomic transition, the data are taken from the atomic line database at
University of Kentucky (van Hoof 2018, https://www.pa.uky.edu/~peter/newpage/).
For the molecular hydrogen, the data of Roueff et al. (2019) are used.  We adopt the 
CO and HD data from the HITRAN (Gordon et al. 2022, https://hitran.org). For CH+, 
the data of Changala et al. (2021) are used.  The data of OH are taken from Tabone 
et al. (2021) and Zannese et al. (2024) using Yousefi et al. (2018) and Brooke et al. (2016).  
For C60, the data in Brieva et al. (2016) are taken.  The data of Aromatic Infrared Bands (AIBs)
shortwards of 17um are taken from Peeters et al. (2023) and Chown et al. (2023) while 
the data of Aromatic Infrared Bands (AIBs) longwards of 17um  from Smith et al. (2007).  
For the data of ice species, Gibb et al. (2004) and Boogert et al. (2015) are used.
